University Orientation Day

The university president speaks at an orientation day for new students about our university and the various methods the university will provide to prepare its students for the job market. This step reflects the university's commitment to the importance of their specialization and its pivotal role in serving national projects.

Discipline and Behavior

The official spokesperson for the university president discussed the code of discipline and behavior that students must follow throughout the academic year, highlighting the importance of adhering to these rules to ensure a successful and happy academic year. He also emphasized the penalties for failing to comply with these regulations.

Sports Day

As part of the college's ongoing role in student development and investing in the knowledge and athletic growth of our promising youth, our university organized a sports day aimed at providing psychological support to students in an integrated manner, highlighting the importance of both the academic and athletic aspects.

Preparing The University Theater

Organizing and preparing the university theater to welcome the new academic year. The university theater has been organized and prepared to receive the new academic year and the event that will unfold throughout the year. The signing of a scientific and academic cooperation agreement with the Higher Council for Scientific Research.

Organizing a Career Fair

This important event, where all companies present the available job and training opportunities, is considered a chance to introduce the university's students to the job market in the industrial and technological sectors. We always strive to provide students with technical and practical education to qualify them for the job market.

Graduation Ceremony

The university celebrated the graduation of fourth-year students in a joyful ceremony held at the university's outdoor courtyard. This event was a recognition of the students' achievements throughout their time at the university and served as an encouragement for all students to continue striving and working hard.
